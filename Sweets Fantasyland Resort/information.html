Resort At A Glance

Welcome to Sweets Fantasyland Resort.

Sweets Fantasyland Resort is a paradise for sweet tooth. It is located on a small island floating in the fairy-floss clouds. We not only provide comfortable accommodation, but also offer several special activities, including sweets party, dessert making and designing, and lazy river ride. Therefore, Sweets Fantasyland Resort is a great place for teenagers to learn how to make and design desserts in a casual environment in summer, and for couples and family with children to spend their vacation.
